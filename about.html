A short introduction

Originally a teacher for Latin and French, I decided to not stop at that but continue learning in different fields and working as a class teacher at an elementary school and then a progressive education secondary school for putting greater focus on pupil’s individual needs.

I developed a deeper interest in programming while participating in a workshop on becoming an IT teacher. Afterwards I continued expanding my knowledge by attending the two day workshop New Devs on the block at Zollhof as well as the ngGirls workshop and NG-DE Angular conference in Berlin.
Right now I am looking into the area of web developing using HTML, CSS and Javascript.
