Pappenheim, 13/12/2019

School trip to Pappenheim

It is the last day of our school trip. For a week we stayed in Pappenheim, a small town with an old castle in the far south of Nuremberg. We enjoyed ourselves a lot, even did a night hike under a full moon with only small candles in hand and of course a little ghost story had to be told.

Nuremberg, 24/10/2019

One week trip to Helsinki, Finland

Finally it is time for our annual trip to Finland. On saturday we are leaving by plane from Munich airport, It is my first time flying from Munich and I heard lots of good things about the airport. We will be staying at an AirBnB in the very centre of the city, near Uspenski cathedral and Helsinki cathedral. So we will be exploring most of the capital by foot. Hopefully we will be seeing some nothern lights.
